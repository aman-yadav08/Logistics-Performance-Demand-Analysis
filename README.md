# Logistics Performance & Demand Analysis

## 📊 Project Overview

This project analyzes logistics operations, shipping efficiency, and regional demand using an interactive Power BI dashboard. The goal is to identify delivery performance trends, shipping delays, and regional sales distribution to support better supply chain decision-making.

The dashboard was built using a dataset containing **180,000+ order records**, enabling insights into delivery performance, customer segments, shipping modes, and geographic demand.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze logistics and delivery performance
* Identify shipping delays and late delivery trends
* Evaluate demand across regions and product categories
* Understand customer purchasing patterns
* Visualize supply chain performance using interactive dashboards

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

* **SQL** – Data cleaning and preprocessing
* **Excel** – Initial data exploration and formatting
* **Power BI** – Data visualization and dashboard development

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### 1️⃣ Overview

Provides a high-level summary of logistics performance.

Key metrics:

* Total Orders
* Total Sales
* Average Delivery Time
* Late Delivery Rate

Visualizations:

* Average Delivery Time by Shipping Mode
* Delivery Status Distribution
* Orders by Customer Segment

---

### 2️⃣ Shipping Analysis

Focuses on shipping efficiency and delivery performance.

Key metrics:

* Average Shipping Time
* Scheduled Shipping Time
* Late Deliveries
* On-time Deliveries

Visualizations:

* Actual vs Scheduled Shipping Time by Shipping Mode
* Late Delivery Rate
* Late Deliveries Trend by Year
* Orders by Department

---

### 3️⃣ Regional Insights

Analyzes geographic demand and sales performance.

Key metrics:

* Total Countries
* Total Cities
* Total Profit

Visualizations:

* Sales by Region
* Global Sales Distribution (Map)
* Orders by Product Category
* Top Countries by Sales

---

## 📂 Dataset

The dataset contains logistics and order information including:

* Order ID
* Shipping Mode
* Delivery Status
* Sales
* Customer Segment
* Product Category
* Region and Country
* Delivery Time Metrics

Total records analyzed: **~180,000 rows**

---

## 📊 Key Insights

* Certain shipping modes experience higher delivery delays.
* Consumer customers contribute the highest order volume.
* Sales demand varies significantly across global regions.
* Specific product categories generate the majority of orders.
